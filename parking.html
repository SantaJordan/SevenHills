Parking

We are located on the Hyde street cable car line between Fisherman's Wharf and Downtown.

Street parking is very difficult in our neighborhood. If you do decide to drive, the closest parking option is the Jug Shop on the corner of Polk and Pacific ($10 nightly flat rate). Please allow at least fifteen minutes to park and walk. From the Jug Shop, walk two blocks up Pacific avenue, we are on the right at Hyde street.

We highly recommend taking the cable car or a cab so that you you don't have to worry about parking -- and can experience all that our wine list has to offer.
