Parking


We are located on the Hyde street cable car line between Fisherman's Wharf and Downtown.

Street parking in the neighborhood is often difficult. Parking is available in the parking lot of The Jug Shop at Polk and Pacific, 1590 Pacific Avenue, San Francisco, CA 94109, (415) 885-2922. It's just two blocks from Seven Hills. While it is not advertisied, parking at The Jug Shop costs $10 and should be paid to the cashier inside the store. From The Jug Shop, walk two blocks east (away from Polk St.) on Pacific, turn right on Hyde, and Seven Hills is on your left.

Please allow at least fifteen minutes to park and walk.

We highly recommend taking the cable car or a cab so that you you don't have to worry about parking -- and so that you can experience all that our wine list has to offer.
